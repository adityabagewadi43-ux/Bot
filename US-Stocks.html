What Are Stocks?

Stocks represent partial ownership in publicly traded companies listed on exchanges like NYSE and NASDAQ. Owning shares gives you proportional rights to assets, earnings, and voting power.

Capital appreciation (price growth)
Dividend payments (profit sharing)
Voting rights on corporate matters
Liquidity through organized exchanges

How Markets Operate

Stock exchanges facilitate continuous trading through electronic order books matching buyers and sellers. Prices reflect real-time supply/demand dynamics influenced by earnings, news, and macroeconomics.
