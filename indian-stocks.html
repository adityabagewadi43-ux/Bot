📖 What are Stocks?

Stocks represent ownership in a company. When you buy shares of a company, you become a partial owner and can benefit from its growth through price appreciation and dividends.

In India, stocks are traded mainly on the National Stock Exchange (NSE) and Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). Popular indices include Nifty 50 and Sensex.

🏛️ Indian Market Structure

  • Primary Market (IPO)
  • Secondary Market (Stock Trading)
  • Regulator: SEBI
  • Depositories: NSDL & CDSL
  • Regulated
  • Transparent
  • Electronic Trading

⚙️ How Stock Market Works

Investors buy and sell shares through brokers. Prices change based on demand and supply. Orders are matched electronically on exchanges like NSE and BSE.
