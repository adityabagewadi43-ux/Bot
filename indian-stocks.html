📖 What are Stocks?

Stocks represent ownership in a company. When you buy shares, you become a partial owner and can benefit from growth and dividends.

🏛️ Indian Market Structure

  • Primary Market (IPO)
  • Secondary Market
  • Regulated by SEBI
  • Depositories: NSDL & CDSL

⚙️ How Market Works

Investors trade stocks through brokers. Prices change based on demand and supply on NSE and BSE.
