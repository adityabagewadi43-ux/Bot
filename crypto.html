🪙 What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital form of money that operates independently of central banks. It uses cryptographic techniques to secure transactions and control the creation of new units. Unlike traditional currencies, cryptocurrencies are decentralized and run on blockchain networks.

The most popular cryptocurrency is Bitcoin (BTC), introduced in 2009. Since then, thousands of cryptocurrencies have been developed, each with unique features and use cases.

🔗 Blockchain Technology

Blockchain is a distributed ledger recording transactions across a network.

  • Transaction data
  • Timestamp
  • Hash of previous block
  • Secure
  • Transparent
  • Decentralized

⚙️ How Cryptocurrency Works

Transactions are verified by miners/validators using Proof of Work or Proof of Stake and permanently stored on blockchain.
